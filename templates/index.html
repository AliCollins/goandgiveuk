Go and Give

Bringing together a group of atheletes to log their activities for a charitable challenge

  • Sign Up

Log your activities using a sports tracker or app
to work together to complete a challenge

Your sports activities are not just about you. By joining together with your friends and colleagues, you can achieve so much more!
As a team, challenge yourselves to run around the world, or climb Everest, swim across the Atlantic or cycle across America. It may seems impossible on your own...but together you can do great things!
And it's not just about you. Work together to raise money for a charitable cause, allowing colleagues, friends and family to see what you are doing...and to help make others lives better.

Use your own tracker

You do not need any special sports tracker or mobile app to make this work. We can bring together your data...and only the activities you want to share...with that recorded by the other team members. No changes are made to your records.

All your effors can count

It doesn't matter whether you ran for two hours or two minutes - your every effort helps to meet the goal.

Encourage one another

This isn't just about individual effort. The goal belongs to the team...and you all achieve it together. Use the messenger boards to encourage your fellow atheletes as you strive to meet each target.

Targets and rewards!

Sometimes encouragement can be more than just a kind message. Set up specific individual targets, and send "rewards" to those that complete them!

For your team

No matter what your team does - work, social, sports, etc - sign up and create challenges that will inspire you to keep on going, build your team...and get fitter as you do it!
